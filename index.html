About

Music made as an offering

Soulful Music India began as a simple act of devotion — turning quiet morning prayers into recorded music that anyone could carry into their own day. Every bhajan, aarti and mantra on this channel is an original composition, written and produced to sit comfortably in a daily puja, a meditation practice, or just a moment of stillness in a loud day.

The catalogue moves between traditions on purpose — Krishna's playful love, Shiva's stark stillness, Hanuman's steady strength, Ganesh's clearing of obstacles — alongside longer meditative pieces built for sitting, breathing, and simply being. The thread running through all of it is the same: music as a doorway back to the soul.
